Dedicated to Quality & Trust

Welcome to Goodwin Traders, your premier destination for high-quality Sri Lankan rice. Rooted in the heart of Polonnaruwa, we have spent over a decade building relationships with local farmers to bring you the finest harvest.

Our mission is simple: to bridge the gap between traditional farming excellence and modern distribution, ensuring that every grain of rice that reaches your table is pure, authentic, and sustainably sourced.
